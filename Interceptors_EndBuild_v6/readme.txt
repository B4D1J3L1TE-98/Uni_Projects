Interceptors: Biosecurity readme

Ensure that the selected resolution is 1920x1080 (this may require the checking or unchecking of the ‘Display’ checkbox), and the selected monitor is Display 1.

Game controls are explained in the game’s tutorial, accessible from the main menu, but are also provided here.

Universal Controls:
Esc - Quit the game and return to main menu
Space - Pause/Unpause the game

Boat Chase Controls:
Move with WASD or the arrow keys (W or Up vs S or Down for forwards and backwards respectively, A vs Left or D vs Right for turning left and right respectively)
Board boats with the enter/return key, or with either shift key.

Inspection controls:
Move to the locations of the flashing green squares by clicking on them. 
Rotate your viewing perspective (the camera) by clicking the arrows around the edge of the screen.
Open/Close boxes by clicking their lids.
Click a marine life specimen to examine it, and rotate it with WASD or the arrow keys. Identify it as either ‘legal’ or ‘legal’ by clicking the respectively marked boxes.
View the book of marine life by clicking the ‘Open Book’ button in the top right hand corner of the screen.
Exit the boat by clicking the ‘Exit Boat’ button in the top left hand corner.
